About MNC

Redefining Creative Innovation

Understanding creativity beyond traditional capitalist frameworks

01

Traditional View

Creativity as intense concentration and contemplation—an intrinsic, individual activity for those with time, money, and resources.

02

Alternative Perspective

Creative practices emerge within situations of hardship, where there is a lack of time, money, and resources.

03

MNC Framework

A creative process that relies on networked resources and mobility within environments connecting people and technologies.

"In the Zaatari refugee camp, Safwan Harb created an electric bike from spare parts, making mobility possible by re-creating himself alongside the mobile-wheelchair/bike."

— Field Study, Jordan 2024
